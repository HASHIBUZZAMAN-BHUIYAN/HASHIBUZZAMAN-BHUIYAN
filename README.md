## About Me

I am a developer, creator and innovator with a strong focus on building intelligent systems that connect software with the physical world. My work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, embedded hardware and robotics, and I am driven by one simple goal: turning complex ideas into real, working solutions.

I work extensively with Nvidia Jetson platforms to deploy AI and computer vision models directly on compact devices. I build systems that can detect objects, interpret visual data and make decisions in real time, all without relying on cloud infrastructure. These systems are optimized, reliable and built for real world conditions.

On the embedded systems side, I use Raspberry Pi to design IoT automation tools, sensor networks, remote monitoring platforms and custom hardware controllers. I believe that strong engineering is not defined by the cost of the tools, but by the quality of the thinking behind them.

Agentic AI is an area I am deeply invested in. I design autonomous AI systems and multi agent architectures that can perceive their environment form a plan and act independently. I am particularly interested in how large language models can be combined with real world tools to create agents that are genuinely useful and adaptive.

Robotics brings all these interests together. I work on robotic arm control, locomotion systems, simulation environments including Isaac Lab, and the integration of AI perception with physical actuation. My goal is to make robots that are not only functional but intelligent and responsive to dynamic real-world environments.

Every project in this profile is something I designed, built, tested and improved myself. I do not just write code. I find problems and build solutions.
